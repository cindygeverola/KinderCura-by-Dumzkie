About KinderCura

KinderCura is a web-based early childhood development monitoring system designed to help parents and pediatricians work together in screening, tracking, reviewing, and supporting child development through one connected platform.

For Parents

Parents can register their child, answer the developmental screening, view assessment results, receive recommendations, and book appointments with pediatricians.

For Pediatricians

Pediatricians can review child results, approve appointments, create custom questions, send diagnosis and recommendations, and track patient progress over time.

For Admin

The admin monitors accounts, approves pediatrician access, uploads training datasets, and views reports and analytics for the whole system.
